LittleHands Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

JG Labs ("we", "our", "us") built LittleHands as a commercial app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

LittleHands is designed with privacy in mind. We collect as little data as possible.

Location Data

The watchOS app requests your approximate location solely to display current weather conditions on the watch face. Location data is sent directly to Apple's WeatherKit service to retrieve weather information. We do not store, log, or transmit your location data to any server.

Photos

The iOS app allows you to select photos from your library to create custom watch face clock hands. Photos are processed entirely on your device. We do not upload, store, or access your photos on any external server.

Purchase Information

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information.

Data Storage

All app data (preferences, profiles, and processed images) is stored locally on your device and synced between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's built-in Watch Connectivity framework. No data is stored on external servers.

Third-Party Services

LittleHands uses the following Apple services:

  • Apple WeatherKit — to provide weather data on the watch face. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — to process in-app purchases.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

LittleHands does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app displays photos that parents choose to add and does not require account creation or personal data entry.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@jglabs.app.
